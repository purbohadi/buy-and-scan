Scan & Parse

Scan & Parse is a web app for travelers and shoppers: you photograph a paper receipt, the service suggests line items and totals using AI, you review and edit, then you can save copies in a database and append rows to a Google Sheet in your own Google Drive when you connect Google.

Why we ask for Google data: Sign in with Google gives us your account id and email so we can keep your receipts separate from other users. If you use Connect Google Drive & Sheets, we ask for permission only to create and update files this app creates in your Drive (not your whole Drive) and to update that spreadsheet—so approved receipts can appear in your sheet.
